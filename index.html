About Victorious Song Contest

The Victorious Song Contest is a monthly online music competition where fictional countries compete for the title of the best song. Each country submits a song, and fans across the world vote to decide the winner!

Fun Facts

  • The contest was created as a way to unite music lovers in fictional settings.
  • Each month, a new fictional host city is chosen based on the previous winner's country.
  • There are around 20 fictional nations competing in the Victorious Song Contest.
